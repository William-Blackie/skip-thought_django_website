Skippy is a final year project used for the creation of extractive summaries based on the research implementation of Ryan Kiros.

The aims of Skippy is to create informative summarise in English from either .txt or web URLS. click here to get started.

Skippy has first trained after a selection of 20,000 of English Wikipedias most frequent words on a corpus of around 1 million sentences for approximately two weeks and has shown to improve on semantics of summaries produced from a small user survey conducted by myself.
